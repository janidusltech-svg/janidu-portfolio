How to Improve Q2 Reading

  • For difficult MCQs, remove options that are too broad, too strong, or not mentioned.
  • For matching headings, ask: “What is the whole paragraph mainly doing?”
  • For completion questions, check the word limit and copy the word form from the passage.
  • For True/False/Not Given, use only the passage.
