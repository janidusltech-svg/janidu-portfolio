Last-Minute Revision Advice

  • For True/False/Not Given, check whether the passage confirms, contradicts, or does not mention the idea.
  • For MCQ, underline the keyword in the question, then find the same meaning in the passage.
  • For Matching Headings, read the whole paragraph and choose the main idea, not one small detail.
  • For Completion questions, copy words exactly and check the word limit.
